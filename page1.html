WHO WE ARE

Jimbel Nwabueze foundation is a Christian non-profit organization that is committed to showing the love of Christ to the world by establishing platforms that would bring about the change to the economy of Africa as well as the continent and by ripple effect and the rest of the world. We as an organization are committed to revealing the true love of Jesus Christ to the world through our progress and in everything we do.
